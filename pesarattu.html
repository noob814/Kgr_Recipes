Pesarattu

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Green moong dal (lentils)
  • Green chilies
  • Salt, Oil for cooking
  • Ginger, garlic
  • Chutney (coconut or ginger chutney)

Nutrients:

  • Protein: Green moong dal is a great source of plant-based protein
  • Vitamins: Ginger and green chilies offer antioxidants and anti-inflammatory benefits.
  • Fiber: Lentils provide fiber that aids digestion.

Health Benefits:

  • Supports muscle repair with protein.
  • Aids digestion and keeps the gut healthy with fiber.
  • Promotes weight loss by providing a low-calorie, high-nutrient meal.

Process

Pesarattu is made by grinding green moong dal (lentils) with green chilies, ginger, and a bit of salt. The mixture is made into a batter, similar to dosa batter. Heat a tawa (griddle) and spread the batter into a thin pancake, then cook it with oil on both sides.
Serve hot with ginger chutney or coconut chutney.
