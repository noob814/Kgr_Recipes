Paneer Butter Masala

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Paneer
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Cashew paste
  • Spices: Garam masala, red chili powder

Nutrients:

  • Protein: Paneer is a rich source of high-quality protein.
  • Healthy Fats: Butter and cream provide essential fats.
  • Vitamins: Tomatoes offer Vitamin C and antioxidants like lycopene.

Health Benefits:

  • Supports muscle growth due to its protein content.
  • Lycopene helps reduce oxidative stress.
  • Provides energy through healthy fats and carbohydrates.

Process

To make Paneer Butter Masala, saut√© onions, tomatoes, and spices like cumin, garam masala, and coriander powder in butter. Once the mixture is soft, add cream and blend it into a smooth sauce. Then, add cubed paneer to the sauce and cook until well coated.
Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with naan or rice.
