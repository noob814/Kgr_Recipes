Chettinad Chicken Curry

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger, garlic
  • Spices: Garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder, fresh curry leaves

Nutrients:

  • Protein: Chicken provides high-quality protein.
  • Vitamins: Tomatoes and garlic offer vitamins like Vitamin C.
  • Healthy Fats: The oil used in the curry contains essential fats..

Health Benefits:

  • Provides protein for muscle growth and repair.
  • Rich in antioxidants from garlic and tomatoes, supporting immune health.
  • Aids digestion with the help of spices like fennel and ginger.

Process

For Chettinad Chicken Curry, marinate chicken with turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. In a pan, roast a mixture of spices like fennel seeds, cinnamon, cloves, and dry coconut until aromatic. Grind this roasted mixture into a paste. In a separate pan, saut√© onions, tomatoes, and garlic, then add the ground masala paste. Cook the chicken in this mixture, adding water to adjust the consistency. Garnish with fresh curry leaves and serve hot with rice or paratha.
