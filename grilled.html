Grilled Chicken with Veggies

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic, lemon
  • Bell peppers, zucchini, onions
  • Spices: Salt and pepper

Nutrients:

  • Protein: Chicken provides lean protein.
  • Vitamins: Vegetables like bell peppers are rich in Vitamin C.
  • Healthy Fats: Olive oil provides heart-healthy fats.

Health Benefits:

  • Supports muscle repair with protein.
  • The vitamins in vegetables boost immunity and skin health.
  • Healthy fats support heart health.

Process

Season chicken breasts with olive oil, garlic, lemon, salt, and pepper. Grill until cooked through. Toss a variety of vegetables like bell peppers, zucchini, and onions with olive oil and seasonings, then grill them until tender.
Serve the chicken alongside the grilled veggies
