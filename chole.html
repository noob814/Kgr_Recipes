Chole Curry

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Ginger, garlic
  • Spices: Garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric, coriander powder

Nutrients:

  • Protein: Chickpeas are an excellent source of plant-based protein.
  • Vitamins: Chickpeas provide iron and folate.
  • Fiber: High fiber content in chickpeas aids digestion.

Health Benefits:

  • Supports muscle repair with protein.
  • Promotes digestion and gut health due to fiber.
  • Provides iron for healthy blood circulation.

Process

Soak chickpeas overnight and cook them in a pressure cooker with water and spices. For the curry base, saut√© onions, tomatoes, ginger, garlic, and spices like cumin, coriander, and garam masala. Once the tomatoes soften, add the cooked chickpeas and cook until the flavors combine.
Garnish with cilantro and serve with bhature or rice.
