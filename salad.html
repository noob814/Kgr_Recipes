Caesar Salad

Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Grilled chicken
  • Croutons
  • Ginger, garlic
  • Caesar dressing (mayonnaise, garlic, anchovies, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, Parmesan)

Nutrients:

  • Protein: Chicken provides lean protein
  • Healthy Fats: The dressing adds healthy fats from mayonnaise and cheese.
  • Fiber: Lettuce provides fiber for digestion.

Health Benefits:

  • Helps build and repair tissues with protein from chicken.
  • Fiber from lettuce aids digestion and supports gut health.
  • The healthy fats in the dressing help maintain energy and regulate hormones.
